About Us


Our Story

Most of us have been stuck in an airport a time or two and wanted a way that we could better select our origin and/or layover airports based on how stressful they prove to be. We did this using open api data from the Bureau of Transportation showing historical TSA wait times as well as airline delays. Using our Stress-O-Meter can aid in better decision making power when it comes to airport selection.
